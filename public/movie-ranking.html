Argueably A List Of The Best Movies of All Time

My Top 3 (Not in order)

#1. The Incredible Hulk

The Incredible Hulk is a 2008 American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics character the Hulk.It is the second film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) and a reboot of the Hulk film series, following Ang Lee's Hulk (2003). Directed by Louis Leterrier, the film stars Edward Norton as Bruce Banner / Hulk,
Liv Tyler as Betty Ross,
Tim Roth as Emil Blonsky / Abomination,
William Hurt as Thaddeus Ross,
and Tim Blake Nelson as Samuel Sterns.

#2. Avengers: Endgame

Avengers: Endgame is a 2019 American superhero film based on the Marvel Comics superhero team the Avengers.
It is the direct sequel to Avengers: Infinity War (2018) and the 22nd film in the Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU).
Directed by Anthony and Joe Russo, the film features an ensemble cast including Robert Downey Jr., Chris Evans, Mark Ruffalo, Chris Hemsworth, Scarlett Johansson, Jeremy Renner, Don Cheadle, Paul Rudd, Brie Larson, Karen Gillan, Danai Gurira, Benedict Wong, Bradley Cooper, and Josh Brolin.

#3. Spiderman 3

Spiderman 3 is a 2007 superhero film directed by Sam Raimi, starring Tobey Maguire as Peter Parker/Spider-Man.
It is the third installment in Sam Raimi's Spiderman trilogy, following Spiderman (2002) and Spiderman 2 (2004).
The film explores Peter's struggle with his darker side after coming into contact with an alien symbiote, while facing new villains Sandman and Venom.
It features action-packed sequences, emotional conflicts, and the culmination of storylines from the previous films in the trilogy.
